Collect The Crystals

Below you will see all the crystals you have to collect.

Each Crystal is worth a different point value, which you will only see reflected in your score. Thus, you will have to remember each crystal's value as you play.

The object of the game is to match the random number score by collecting the right crystals. If your score at the end of each round equals the random number, you win. If your score exceeds the random number value, you lose.

Click on the crystals until your score matches the random number.
